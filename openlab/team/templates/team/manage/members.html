Manage members

From this page, you can manage the members of this team. Members of the team get the same privileges as you: whatever Projects that this team owns or is given access to, all of its members can access.

Additionally, team members can invite other members, upload photos to the team media gallery, write team news posts, and manage the team profile settings.
