New to Open Lab?

Here's some information about project creation.

  • The final URL of a project is in the format openlab.org/name/slug, where name is name of the responsible organization or user.
  • The slug is generated from the title.
  • After the project is (somewhat) complete, including a photograph of a physical prototype of the project, you can publish a 1.0 "Release" of the project.
  • A Release indicates a mature product, and earns prominent placement on Open Lab.

Are you part of a team?

If you're part of a organization, first register a team with Open Lab and add your teammates to it. This will allow multiple people to easily collaborate on the project.
